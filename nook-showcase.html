Why Nook

A problem hiding in plain sight.

Millions of talented home cooks in Indian housing societies have no platform built for them. Nook changes that.

🍱

Home kitchens have no storefront

Talented cooks in your building take orders over WhatsApp — no menu, no payments, no tracking, no analytics.

📍

Hyperlocal means truly local

Zomato and Swiggy are built for restaurants. Society food is different — shorter distances, trusted cooks, home-style meals.

📅

Pre-orders don't fit anywhere

Tiffin services, festive platters, and weekly meal plans need scheduled ordering — a flow no food-delivery app handles well.

💳

Payment is chaos

UPI screenshots, cash on delivery, and "I'll pay later" are the norm. Nook brings structured online payments and COD options together.

Features

Built for every stakeholder.

Three distinct portals — each purpose-built for its user.

📍

Location-aware discovery

Auto-detects your society using GPS and Nominatim geocoding. Shows kitchens by proximity — in your society, nearby, or broader radius.

⚡

Spot & pre-orders

Order food that's ready now (spot) or schedule ahead for tiffin and meal prep windows (pre-order) — two entirely different flows handled elegantly.

🚚

Delivery or pickup

Choose delivery to your flat or self-pickup. Delivery address, flat number, floor, and landmark — all captured at checkout.

💳

Flexible payments

Pay online via Razorpay, cash on delivery, or online on delivery — whatever the kitchen accepts, the buyer can choose.

📦

Live order tracking

Status timeline from placement to delivery, with full cancel-before-accept support and push notifications at every step.

🔔

Push notifications

Web push keeps buyers informed on acceptance, dispatch, and delivery — works even when the browser is closed (PWA).

📋

Full menu management

Create item groups (categories), add items with photos, pricing, and descriptions. Toggle availability in one tap. Drag to reorder.

🕐

Flexible order windows

Define spot ordering hours per day. Create multiple pre-order windows with custom open/close and delivery times — ideal for tiffin services.

✅

Order workflow

Accept, dispatch, or reject orders with structured rejection reasons. Auto-cancellation if no response within the configured window.

📊

Analytics dashboard

Revenue trends, order funnel, acceptance rates, top items by count and revenue, cancellation breakdown — all with date-range filtering.

🗺

Delivery zone control

Set delivery scope to society-only (within 500m) or a custom radius. Configure separate delivery fees for spot vs. pre-orders.

🔔

Instant new-order alerts

Push notifications fire the moment an order lands — so sellers never miss a customer even if the tab is in the background.

📈

Platform GMV dashboard

Gross Merchandise Value broken down by today, this month, and all-time — the top-line number leadership cares about.

🏪

Kitchen oversight

Total and active kitchen count, store-level visibility, commission configuration, and onboarding status.

👥

User management

Buyers vs. sellers breakdown, user lookup API, and role-based access control enforced across all admin routes.

🗂

Live order feed

Real-time view of the last 10 platform orders — kitchen, buyer, status, amount, timestamp — to monitor operational health.
